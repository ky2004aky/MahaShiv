Significance

Why We Celebrate

Maha Shivratri holds profound spiritual significance. It is believed that on this night, the northern hemisphere is positioned in a way that naturally raises human energy‚Äîmaking it ideal for spiritual practices.

Devotees stay awake through the night, chanting "Om Namah Shivaya" and performing abhishekam to honor the destroyer of ignorance and the bestower of liberation.

Sacred Practices

02

Stay awake through the night in devotion, singing bhajans and chanting mantras.

Chant "Om Namah Shivaya" 108 times for spiritual purification.
